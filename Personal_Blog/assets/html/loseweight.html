What is the correct way to measure weight?




Measuring the weight correctly is easy, but it is important because there are many factors that affect the correctness of the measurement. Here you will find the correct way to measure the weight. The restriction in these steps will give the most accurate result of the measurement of weight.

First :

the time The correct time that you must limit is when you wake up, then the body is free of food and drink, and also after entering the bathroom, that is, you cannot measure your weight in the afternoon or evening period and after food or even after drinking water because all this will increase the number on the miran.

Secondly :

the clothing Your clothes should be very light, or you can put the scale in the bathroom. You will also measure your weight after entering the bathroom and before taking a shower. Clothes, even if you say they have weight, will affect the validity of the number on the scale.

Third :

The scale used for measurement Changing the type of scale affects the validity of the number, because the scales are not the same accuracy, and you will get less weight in one scale and more weight in another scale, so rely on one scale.
